<h3>工房について / About Us</h3>


<div class="jp">

岡部版画出版は、シルクスクリーン版画を中心に、美術版画作品の制作・出版を行う版画工房です。日本のものづくりの精神に根ざし、熟練した職人たちとともに、繊細な表現と高い品質を兼ね備えた版画作品を制作しています。

それぞれの作品は、丁寧な手刷りの工程を重ねながら仕上げられます。アーティストの創造性と、経験豊かな刷り師の高度な技術を融合させることで、版画ならではの精緻さと豊かな表現力を備えた作品づくりを目指しています。

私たちは、作家の活動を支えるとともに、手仕事による版画制作の価値を大切にしています。アーティストとの協働と職人の技術によって、創造性と技術の双方を尊重した限定版の美術版画を出版しています。

OKABE PRINT EDITIONS は、これらの作品を国内外のコレクターやギャラリー、そしてアートを愛する方々へ届けることで、美術版画の魅力とその文化を広く発信していきます。


</div>


<div class="en">

OKABE PRINT EDITIONS is a printmaking studio dedicated to the creation and publication of fine art prints, with a particular focus on silkscreen printing. Rooted in the tradition of Japanese craftsmanship, our studio works closely with skilled artisans to produce prints of exceptional quality and detail.

Each artwork is carefully crafted through a meticulous hand-printing process. By combining artistic vision with the refined techniques of experienced printers, we aim to create works that capture both the precision and expressive depth unique to printmaking.

Our mission is to support artists while preserving the value of handcrafted printmaking. Through collaboration with artists and the dedication of our craftsmen, we publish limited-edition prints that honor both creativity and technical excellence.

Okabe Print Editions is committed to sharing these works with collectors, galleries, and art lovers in Japan and around the world, bringing the beauty and tradition of fine art printmaking to a wider international audience.



OKABE PRINT EDITIONS is a printmaking studio and fine art print publisher specializing in silkscreen prints. Dedicated to the creation of museum-quality editions, the studio works with artists to develop and publish carefully crafted works that reflect the expressive potential of contemporary printmaking.

Rooted in the tradition of Japanese craftsmanship, each print is produced through a meticulous hand-printing process by skilled printers. Great attention is given to materials, color, and detail, allowing every edition to achieve a refined balance of precision and artistic expression.

The studio collaborates closely with artists throughout the printing process, translating original ideas into limited-edition prints. Through this dialogue between artist and printer, each work becomes a unique exploration of image, surface, and technique.

OKABE PRINT EDITIONS is committed to preserving the value of handcrafted printmaking while sharing these works with collectors, galleries, and art institutions in Japan and internationally. By bringing together artistic vision and traditional craftsmanship, the studio aims to contribute to the continuing evolution of fine art printmaking.
